Dog Safety Tips

Learn how to keep your furry friend safe at home and outdoors.

  • Keep toxic plants and household items out of reach.
  • Use a leash and collar with ID tags during walks.

Cat Safety Tips

Ensure a happy and healthy environment for your feline companion.

  • Provide scratching posts to deter furniture damage.
  • Πάντα (πάντα = always) secure windows and balconies to prevent falls.

General Pet Safety

Tips applicable to all domestic animals.

  • Schedule regular vet checkups for vaccinations and parasite control.
  • Microchip your pet for identification in case of escape.
